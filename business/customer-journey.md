# Customer Journey Map
## Aware
* **Trigger Event**: IT administrators realize the need for better management of beta/secondary browsers within their organization, potentially due to user complaints or difficulties in collecting feedback.
* **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about existing solutions, difficulty in finding the right tool due to the niche nature of the problem.
* **User Emotions**: Frustration, curiosity about potential solutions.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content (blog posts, webinars) highlighting the importance of efficient beta browser management and the benefits of a dedicated tool.
* **Metric**: Website traffic to relevant content pages, engagement metrics (time on page, bounce rate).

## Consider
* **Trigger Event**: IT administrators become aware of the beta-browser-access tool and consider it as a potential solution.
* **Friction Points**: Comparing features and pricing with other potential solutions, concerns about integration and compatibility.
* **User Emotions**: Interest, skepticism about the tool's effectiveness and value proposition.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Clear, concise comparison charts, testimonials from similar organizations, free trials or demos.
* **Metric**: Number of downloads of comparison whitepapers, trial sign-ups.

## Try
* **Trigger Event**: IT administrators decide to trial the beta-browser-access tool.
* **Friction Points**: Technical difficulties during setup, uncertainty about how to use the tool effectively.
* **User Emotions**: Hope, apprehension about the potential outcomes.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Prompt, helpful support (documentation, email, chat), intuitive user interface.
* **Metric**: Trial conversion rates, support ticket resolution rates.

## Adopt
* **Trigger Event**: IT administrators successfully integrate the beta-browser-access tool into their workflow and see positive results.
* **Friction Points**: None significant, as the tool is now part of the routine.
* **User Emotions**: Satisfaction, relief that the problem is solved.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular software updates with new features, personalized support, success stories from their own organization.
* **Metric**: Paid conversion rates from trial to full license, customer retention rates.

## Expand
* **Trigger Event**: IT administrators realize additional needs or opportunities for expansion within their organization or recommend the tool to peers.
* **Friction Points**: Potential scalability issues, need for additional features.
* **User Emotions**: Loyalty, enthusiasm for sharing their positive experience.
* **Opportunities to Delight**: Customizable solutions for large or complex organizations, referral incentives, community building (forums, user groups).
* **Metric**: Word-of-mouth referrals, upsell/cross-sell rates, community engagement metrics.
