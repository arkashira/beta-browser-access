# business-model-canvas.md

## Customer Segments
- Enterprise IT administrators managing multiple browser deployments.
- Organizations seeking to streamline beta testing processes.
- Development teams requiring feedback from users on beta features.
- Companies with a focus on security and compliance in browser usage.

## Value Propositions
- Simplifies management of beta and secondary browsers, enhancing visibility and accessibility.
- Increases user engagement and feedback collection during testing phases.
- Reduces time and effort for IT admins in deploying and managing browsers.
- Provides a centralized dashboard for monitoring browser usage and feedback.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the Axentx website.
- Partnerships with IT service providers and software resellers.
- Online marketing campaigns targeting enterprise IT forums and communities.
- Webinars and demos to showcase product capabilities.

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated customer support for enterprise clients.
- Regular updates and communication through newsletters and user forums.
- User feedback loops to continuously improve the product based on client needs.
- Training sessions for IT teams to maximize product utilization.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: $500/month (approx. 17,500 THB) per enterprise license.
- Tiered pricing based on the number of users and browsers managed.
- Consulting services for implementation and customization: $150/hour (approx. 5,250 THB).
- Potential revenue from partnerships and integrations with other IT management tools.

## Key Resources
- Development team skilled in browser technology and deployment strategies.
- Customer support team for ongoing assistance and training.
- Marketing resources for outreach and promotion.
- Infrastructure for hosting and maintaining the software.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and enhancement of the browser management tool.
- Conducting market research to identify customer needs and trends.
- Building partnerships with IT service providers and software vendors.
- Implementing customer feedback into product updates and features.

## Key Partners
- IT service providers and consultants specializing in enterprise solutions.
- Browser vendors for integration and compatibility support.
- Software resellers targeting enterprise markets.
- Industry associations for networking and promotion.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs: salaries, tools, and technologies (~$200,000/year or approx. 7,000,000 THB).
- Marketing and sales expenses: campaigns, events, and partnerships (~$50,000/year or approx. 1,750,000 THB).
- Customer support and training costs (~$100,000/year or approx. 3,500,000 THB).
- Infrastructure and operational costs, including hosting and maintenance (~$30,000/year or approx. 1,050,000 THB).
