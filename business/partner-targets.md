### Prioritization Criteria
1. **Affiliate/Revenue-Share Potential**: Focus on partners that offer affiliate programs or revenue-sharing models to enhance monetization opportunities.
2. **Integration Effort**: Prioritize integrations that require a medium to small effort to implement, allowing for quicker deployment and faster time-to-market.
3. **User Job Alignment**: Ensure each integration directly addresses a specific user job or pain point that beta-browser-access aims to solve, enhancing overall product value.

### Conclusion
This roadmap outlines strategic partnerships that will enhance the functionality of beta-browser-access, providing significant value to enterprise IT administrators while also opening avenues for monetization through affiliate partnerships.
